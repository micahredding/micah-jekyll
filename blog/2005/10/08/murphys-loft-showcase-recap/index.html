Murphy's Loft Showcase Recap

The Murphy’s Loft showcase was great. We had Rob Hawkins open up, followed by Shelley Raymond (a graduate of the Edgehill Studios Showcase who has an awesome stage presence). Then Seth Harper played an interesting collection of acoustic rock, including a cover song from the Gin Blossoms.

Be sure to check out his song “Kramer’s Wall” on myspace.com/sethharper.

Finally, Wee The People, including Shawn on guitar, and Clara (fresh from touring in Europe) on keyboards, played an awesome alt-pop set.

To wrap it all up, I sang a couple of songs, then drank a coffee milkshake.

-Micah
