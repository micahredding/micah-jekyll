Harold Ford Jr and Bob Corker

I was listening to the radio talk about how the Tennessee Senate election (between Bob Corker and Harold Ford Jr) was a “race issue”, when I passed by some “Women for Ford” on the side of the road. I rolled down my window to hear what they were screaming, and saw a lady beating her sign against the ground, saying “Harold Ford! Harold Ford!”.

Someone else in traffic had their window down too, and they were screaming “Bob Corker! Bob Corker!”

These two individuals just screamed back and forth at each other like this for a while.

And I just wondered at how silly people are willing to be.

-micah
