Furious Pursuit - a great book

Furious Pursuit is a book I just read by Tim King and Frank Martin. And it’s awesome. I’ve gotten my girlfriend to read it, and I’m going to get my parents and friends to read it too.

The book says that God will never let you go.

And you’ve got to let that sink in for a minute, because that’s exactly what it means.

Not: “won’t let you go…for a while.”
Not: “will never let you go…unless…“
Not: “God’s great…but…“

God will never let you go.

Read it.

-micah
